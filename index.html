About Paul Powell

I'm Paul Powell from Maryland. I create music using AI because I genuinely love the sounds it helps me bring into the world — songs that encourage, uplift, and motivate people to feel good about themselves and make positive changes.

To me, AI-generated music is like brainstorming or rough drafts. It's a powerful starting point full of fresh ideas and beautiful possibilities, but I believe human musicians can take these creations to an even higher level. The best music happens when human heart and intention build upon what AI begins.
